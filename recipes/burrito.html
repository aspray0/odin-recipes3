Burrito Recipe

Description

My basic, everyday burrito.

The fulcrum about which I manage my macronutrient intake.

Ingredients:

  • Tortilla
  • Half pound of ground meat
  • Seasoning Mix
  • Rice or Beans
  • Cheese
  • Jalapenos
  • Salsa
    •

    Steps:

    1. Place beans or rice in instant pot with 3x the volume of water and cook for at least 20 minutes.
    2. While the carbs cook, place meat in skillet over medium heat.
    3. Mix in Seasonings, and cook on medium for 10 to 15 minutes, stirring every-so-often.
    4. Turn heat to low after the meat has finished cooking.
    5. Once the carbs have finished cooking, add them to the skillet with the meat and mix.
    6. Add cheese and jalapenos to the skillet and flip until all cheese is melted.
    7. On a plate, lay out your tortilla.
    8. Move fillings from skillet onto Tortilla, centered well.
    9. Lay salsa on top of fillings.
    10. Fold tortilla over the fillings by rolling the tortilla tightly until the tortilla's edge first meets the inside, then fold in the edges and finish rolling.
    11. Wait a few minutes to let the ingredients set and the tortilla stabilize before serving.
