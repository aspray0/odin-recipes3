Steak

Description

A steak cooked to medium-rare via the Sous Vide (Pronounced Sue Veed) method.

Image courtesy of SeriousEats.com.

Ingredients

  • Ribeye Steak
  • Rosemary
  • Garlic Gloves
  • Shallots
  • Kosher Salt
  • Black Peppercorns
  • Canola Oil

Steps

  1. Set sous vide cooker to desired temperature, 129 to 134 degrees fahrenheit for medium-rare.
  2. Season steak generous with salt and freshly ground black pepper.
  3. Place steak in sous vide bag alongside herbs, garlic, and shallots and distribute evenly.
  4. Seal bag and cook for 2.5 hours.
  5. Remove steak from water bath.
  6. In a stainless steel skillet, heat canola oil over the hottest burner setting until it just starts to smoke.
  7. Gently lay steak in skillet with a pair of tongs and cook for 15 to 30 seconds.
  8. After 15 to 30 seconds, flip steak over and sear for another 15 to 30 seconds. Repeat until desired sear has formed.
  9. Serve steak immediately.
