Pizza Recipe

Description

A classic pepperoni pizza. Staple of US Fast Food, this specific image is from a pizzeria in Seattle, Washington.

This pizza, made by Outsider Pizza, is touted as the best in Seattle according to Erin Lodi.

Ingredients:

  • Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Pepperoni Slices
    •

    Steps:

    1. Press dough flat into a circle.
    2. Spread sauce on dough, leaving a fingernail's length of dough uncovered from the perimeter. Less is more here.
    3. Freshly shred the blocked marinara cheese, and lay cheese on top of sauce.
    4. Evenly spread out the pepperoni slices on top of the cheese. Spacing and quantity are up to personal preference.
    5. Place assembled pizza in a 500 degree fahrenheit oven and cook for 7 minutes.
    6. Take pizza out of the oven and let rest for at least a few minutes.
